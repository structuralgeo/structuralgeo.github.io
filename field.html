Muddy Mountains, Nevada, USA (2025)

A wild week measuring permeability across the visually striking, low angle, Muddy Mountain thrust. Moses has described beautiful injection veins in the hanging wall at this site; excellent evidence for fluid overpressure during seismic events. The Aztec sandstone in the footwall is a high permeability unit; we tested whether the permeability of the footwall decreased towards the fault to permit fluid overpressurization during seismic slip.
