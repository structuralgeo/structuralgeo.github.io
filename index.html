Welcome!

Our group studies the mechanics of faults and shear zones from the field- to nano-scale to better understand the earthquake cycle, the movement of tectonic plates, and the structural controls of mineral resources.

Lakehead University is located on the traditional lands of the Fort William First Nation. Our group lives and works on the traditional territory of the Anishinaabeg (including Ojibwe, Odawa, and Pottawatomi nations) and Metis.
