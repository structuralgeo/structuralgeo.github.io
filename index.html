Welcome!

Our group studies the mechanics of faults and shear zones from the field- to nano-scale to better understand the earthquake cycle, the movement of tectonic plates, and the structural controls of mineral resources.

I am recruiting new PhD students to start in August 2025 (applications due January 2025). Please contact me as soon as possible if you are interested in joining our group.
